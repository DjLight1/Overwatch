Game Description

Overwatch is 6 versus 6 objective based first person shooterdeveloped and published by Blizzard Entertainment. It was released in May 2016 for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One. Players on a team work together to secure and defend control points on a map and/or escort a payload across the map in a limited amount of time.

Ranking System

The new tier bracket system for season 2 of competitive mode is now more dependent on skill level rather than the wins and loses you have in the game. You either climb the bracket until reaching a new tier or drop down in rank of the bracket you are currently on. You will not go down in teir if things just aren't working out for you. Your ranking is also dependent on your season 1 rating for the sake of comparison.
